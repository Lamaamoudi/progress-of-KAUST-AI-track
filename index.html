About me

I'm a Computer Science graduate, first in the Class of 2025. This achievement was driven by a genuine curiosity to figure out how things work and a commitment to learning. I enjoy every side of technology, but my real passion is Artificial Intelligence.

I'm fascinated by how AI can solve real-world problems, something I got to experience firsthand through my graduation project, Ameneen: a real-time emergency detection system for Hajj and Umrah pilgrims, built using deep learning, Apache Kafka, and Android development.

I like to keep sharpening my skills, which is why I'm currently part of KAUST Academy's selective AI specialization program, which I will complete in September 2026. I'm always looking for new challenges, and I'm excited to find a place where I can grow, share my knowledge, and build solutions that make a real difference.
